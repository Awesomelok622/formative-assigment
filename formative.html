Causes of climate Change

Humanity’s increased use of fossil fuels

Human uses coal, oil and gas to generate electricity, run cars and other forms of transport, and power manufacturing and industry

Deforestation

Living trees absorb and store carbon dioxide, which can balance the amount of CO2. However, they are overly cut off by wood factories

Increasingly intensive agriculture

This practice emits greenhouse gases like methane and nitrous oxide, which is hazardous to the enviroment and deterioriate the problem caused by climate change

Over-consumption

Your home and use of power, how you move around, what you eat and how much you throw away all contribute to greenhouse gas emissions. So does the consumption of goods such as clothing, electronics and plastics.

Effects of climate Change

Climate change can lead to numerous impacts to our home, environment, as well as species. For instance, our temperature will get warmer and the increasing temperature of the ocean will lead to the melting of the icebergs, e.g The Artic. Also, more severe storms and increased drought will occur. It is high time for people to take things serious and make concrete actions.

Loss of species and habitats

Climate change poses risks to the survival of species on land and in the ocean. These risks increase as temperatures climb. Forest fires, extreme weather and invasive pests and diseases are among many threats. Some species will be able to relocate and survive, but others will not. Melting Glaciers

Increased drought

Study from Climate Communication has also shown that Climate Communication water is becoming scarcer in more regions. Droughts can stir destructive sand and dust storms that can move billions of tons of sand across continents. Deserts are expanding, reducing land for growing food. Many people now face the threat of not having enough water on a regular basis.

More severe storms

The frequency of hurricanes and strong storms are increasing. Research on the extreme weather has showed a map with frequency of disasters around the globe.
